FAQ

Where is my time sheet saved? How do I access it?

The time sheet is saved to a comma separated file (*.csv) to the devices sdcard in a folder called /sdcard/TerpTools/. Connect your device to your desktop, navigate to /sdcard/TerpTools/, and import TerpTimer.CSV into Excel.

How do I access my file?

Connect your device to your desktop computer, navigate to the CSV file on the SD card, and import TerpTimer.CSV into Excel or any other spreadsheet software.

So, this is beta right?

Yes, it's a work in progress. The developer is human and can make mistakes. Please don't depend soley on this utility (yet) for your record keeping.
